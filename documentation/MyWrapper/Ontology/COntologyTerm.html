COntologyTerm

Description | Descendents | Methods (details)

Generic term.

This kind of term represents a generic term that does not have any specific qualification or function, expect to serve as an aggregator or category for other terms.

In this class we enforce the kTAG_TERM Kind(), and we ensure that the term has both the Code() and name in order to have an _IsInited() status.
